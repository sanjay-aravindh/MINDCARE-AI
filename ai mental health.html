About MindCare AI

MindCare AI combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with evidence-based mental health practices to provide accessible, immediate support for mental wellness. Our AI is trained on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) principles and continuously updated by licensed mental health professionals.

We believe that mental health support should be available 24/7, without barriers, and in a safe, non-judgmental environment. Our technology serves as a bridge to professional care while providing immediate coping strategies and emotional support.
